The full flow:

1. Eduardo taps Fritz bubble → writes "vende 5 iphone 13 a juan a 330 del envio de marzo"
2. Fritz searches inventory, finds 5 available iPhone 13s in that lot, resolves "juan" to "Juan Pérez"
3. Fritz opens the Pre-Purchase Modal with full details: client info, items, IMEIs, totals, payment selector
4. Eduardo can tap "Editar" to change price/quantity/payment, or "Confirmar Venta"
5. Fritz executes the sale transaction (atomic), generates invoice, shows confirmation with quick actions
6. Eduardo can tap "Ver factura" or "Otra venta" to keep going
